Our Mission

With over 200 years of combined experience, Integrated Chemical Resources leverages our extensive expertise, resources, and innovative business models to create synergistic value in the regional chemical industry. Our mission is to enhance operational efficiency, foster growth, and deliver sustainable profitability while providing exceptional value to our stakeholders and contributing to the evolution of the chemical industry.

Our Vision

At Integrated Chemical Resources, we envision becoming a leading global force in the chemical industry. Through our unwavering commitment to operational efficiency, growth, and sustainability, we aim to create a thriving ecosystem that fosters innovation, benefits all stakeholders, and sets new standards for excellence in the chemical sector.

Our Values

  • People

    We recognize that our greatest asset is the talent and diversity of our employees, and we work hard to create a work environment that encourages each person to develop to his or her fullest potential.

  • Product

    Our commitment to quality and innovation drives us to develop and deliver superior chemical products and services that meet the changing requirements of our customers and contribute to the industry's development.

  • Process

    We endeavor for continuous improvement in our operations by adopting efficient and sustainable methods that maximize productivity, minimize waste, and optimize resource utilization.

  • Predictability

    By maintaining a strong emphasis on risk management, data-driven decision-making, and robust planning, we ensure consistent and dependable performance across all aspects of our business, thereby fostering the confidence and trust of our stakeholders.

  • Potential

    We are committed to realizing the company's full potential by funding innovative projects and expanding our business in response to emerging market demands.

Cletus Glasener

Cletus Glasener is the Chairman of Crescent Growth Partners, responsible for ensuring the efficient functioning of the Board of Directors and overseeing the leadership team's performance. He is currently serving on the Boards of two private start-up companies. Before joining CGP, he retired after serving 13 years as the Chief Financial Officer at Elbit Systems of America. His experiences include serving as the Principal Financial and Accounting Officer of Vought Aircraft, reporting directly to the Chairman, President, and CEO, and as the CFO of L-3 Vertex. His private equity experience includes a successful turnaround and exit of Collins Industries and serving as the Chairman and CEO of the Thayer Aerospace asset sale and dissolution. Mr. Glasener received his B.A., in Economics, from Washington University. He received his MBA in Business from the University of Missouri. He is a CPA (Texas), CMA, CFM, and CGMA.

George Knight

George Knight is the Chief Executive Officer at Crescent Growth Partners responsible for creating and implementing strategies, together with the senior management team to determine the direction the company will take. Before joining CGP, he served as EVP and Chief Financial Officer at Hexion Inc, Sr VP Finance and Treasurer Momentive Specialty Chemicals Inc. and V.P. Finance & Treasurer at Borden Chemical. He has spent over Twenty-five years working for private equity-owned companies and has extensive experience with M&A and capital market transactions. Mr. Knight spent the first ten years of his career as an auditor with Deloitte & Touche. Mr. Knight received his B.A., in Economics, from Saint Anselm College. He received his M.S. in Accounting from Syracuse University. He is a certified public accountant (inactive).

Dave Barron

Dave Barron is Chief Financial Officer at Crescent Growth Partners, responsible for tracking cash flow and financial planning, analyzing the company's strengths and weaknesses, and proposing strategic directions. Before joining C.G.P., he served as Chief Financial Officer at NASCENT Technology, Chief Financial Officer at PureStar Group, and Managing Director/C.F.O. at Neapco Europe. Dave's expertise lies in Mergers & Acquisitions, Financial & Operational Improvements, Controls & Compliance, and Cash & Cost Management, with International experience. Mr. Barron received his B.A. in Accounting from Rowan University. He received his M.B.A. in Finance from Seton Hall University.

Carlton Chen

Carlton Chen is General Counsel at Crescent Growth Partners, responsible for providing legal support and advice to upper management on relevant legal issues. He is a practicing business lawyer at Kurien Ouellette and Jia Law Group and has an extensive career providing legal services to diverse ventures. Before joining C.G.P., he served in numerous positions, including Vice President, General Counsel, and Secretary at Colt's Manufacturing Company and as legal counsel for Olin Corporation and Sara Lee Corporation. Mr. Chen received his B.A. from Columbia University with Honors in Economics. He received his Juris Doctor (J.D.) as a Root-Tilden Scholar from the New York University School of Law.

Robert Jameson

Robert Jamieson is a chemical industry executive at Crescent Growth Partners, responsible for ensuring that the Board of Directors establishes clear objectives and delivers agreed-upon plans. He will also support and challenge the leadership team to ensure that the Board of Directors conforms to the highest standards of corporate governance and makes concise and timely decisions. Before joining C.G.P., he served as a President & C.E.O. at Quadrant Chemical Corporation, Chief Executive Officer at Canlak Coatings, V.P. of M&A and Integration at Versaflex Companies, Vice President of Accella Performance Materials Inc. and founder of 1357 Partners, an advisory and co-investment partner to private equity firms focused on the specialty chemical markets. Mr. Jamieson is a graduate of Texas A&M University, Class of 1987.

Andi Zaimi

Andi Zaimi, the dynamic CEO at Integrated Chemical Resources, collaborates with senior management to craft and implement strategies that guide the company's future. Previously a director at Actylis - Specialty Chemicals, he excelled in sales, marketing, budgeting, risk management, efficiency measures, and customer satisfaction. As an accomplished professional, Andi leverages his B.S. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Connecticut to drive success in the chemical industry. His forward-thinking approach will be instrumental in steering his team and organization toward growth and innovation.
